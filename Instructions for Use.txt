Getting online and booting up your first notebook:

Navigate to https://morehouse-outreach.illumidesk.com/
If you have a GitHub account linked to your email address, click "Continue with GitHub"
Otherwise, click "Sign Up"
Enter in your main email address and an arbitrary username and password.
Log in. You should see a folder called "morehouse_outreach_julia"
Navigate to that folder and into a folder called "src,"
and click on "julia_introduction.ipynb" to open your first module.
